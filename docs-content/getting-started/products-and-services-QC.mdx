## Welcome to the VaultWares Ecosystem

VaultWares is dedicated to the philosophy of **Privacy first, Security second, Functionality third**. Our ecosystem spans software applications, specialized hardware, and integrated services designed to give users complete control over their digital footprint. We leverage post-quantum cryptography (PQC), client-side encryption, and zero-tracking principles across all our offerings.

Below is the complete catalog of our current and upcoming products and services.

## Services & Add-ons

We offer specialized services to extend the capabilities of our software and hardware.

*   **PQC Client-Based Encrypted Services:** Future-proof your data with Post-Quantum Cryptography applied directly on the client side.
*   **Local AI Models:** Access to privacy-preserving, locally hosted AI models for text, image, and data processing.
*   **Frontend Pentesting Services:** Automated and manual security assessments for web applications.
*   **Realtime Speech-to-Text:** Native applications providing highly accurate, offline transcription.
*   **Curated Adblock & Anti-Fingerprinting:** A proprietary, manually curated adblock list (refined over a year) and advanced anti-fingerprinting algorithms integrated into our network devices.
