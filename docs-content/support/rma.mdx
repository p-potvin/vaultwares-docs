## Overview

The Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) process is used when you need to return a defective or damaged VaultWares hardware product for replacement or repair under warranty, or for end-of-life disposal. This guide walks through the complete RMA process.

## When to file an RMA

File an RMA if:

- A hardware product has a confirmed defect covered under warranty (see [Warranty information](/support/warranty))
- A device arrived damaged (DOA — Dead on Arrival)
- You need to return a device for secure disposal/destruction and receive a certificate of destruction
- You are exchanging a device for a different model or capacity (contact sales for exchanges)

<Note>
  Before filing an RMA, open a support ticket so our team can confirm the defect and issue an RMA number. Packages without a valid RMA number may be refused or returned at your expense.
</Note>

## Step-by-step RMA process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a support ticket">
    Contact VaultWares support to diagnose the issue and confirm warranty coverage:

    - **Portal**: [portal.vaultwares.com/support](https://portal.vaultwares.com/support)
    - **Email**: [support@vaultwares.com](mailto:support@vaultwares.com)
    - **Phone**: Available to Gold and Platinum support plan customers

    Include in your ticket:
    - Device serial number (printed on the device label)
    - Date of purchase and proof of purchase (order number or invoice)
    - Detailed description of the defect
    - Diagnostic output: `vw-admin diag collect --output diag.tar.gz`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive RMA authorization">
    Once the defect is confirmed and warranty coverage is verified, you will receive:

    - **RMA number** (format: `RMA-YYYYMMDD-NNNN`)
    - **RMA instructions** including the return shipping address
    - **Prepaid return shipping label** (for warranty repairs — standard warranty service)
    - **Expected resolution timeline**

    RMA numbers are valid for 30 days from issuance. Contact support to extend if needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Perform crypto-erase before returning (REQUIRED)">
    Before returning any VaultWares hardware, you **must** perform a cryptographic erasure to destroy all key material and data on the device. This protects your organization and is required to maintain your security posture.

    <Warning>
      Returning a device without performing crypto-erase may expose sensitive data. VaultWares will not be responsible for data remaining on returned devices.
    </Warning>

    ```bash
    # Crypto-erase a VaultDrive
    vw-storage crypto-erase \
      --device /dev/sdb \
      --confirm \
      --audit-log "RMA return - RMA-20241115-0042"

    # Remote crypto-erase a managed VaultDrive
    vw-storage remote-crypto-erase \
      --serial VD-ENT-00247 \
      --confirm \
      --reason "RMA-20241115-0042"

    # Erase a VaultHSM (requires SO credentials)
    vw-hsm factory-reset \
      --so-pin <so-pin> \
      --confirm \
      --audit-log "RMA return - RMA-20241115-0043"
    ```

    If the device cannot be powered on or is physically damaged:
    - VaultWares can perform physical destruction and provide a **Certificate of Destruction**
    - Note in your RMA request that the device cannot be erased prior to return
  </Step>

  <Step title="Package the device">
    Proper packaging prevents shipping damage and protects the tamper-evident features of VaultWares hardware.

    **Packaging requirements**:
    - Use the **original VaultWares packaging** if available. If not, use a similarly sized sturdy box with at least 5 cm of foam or bubble wrap on all sides.
    - **Do not** wrap devices directly in loose packing peanuts — they can damage the device and interfere with tamper-detection features.
    - For VaultHSM appliances: rack rails and accessories do not need to be returned unless specifically requested.
    - Include a **printed copy of the RMA number** inside the box.
    - Write the **RMA number** on the outside of the box.

    <Warning>
      Attach the prepaid shipping label to the outside of the box, not the original manufacturer's box. Do not obscure any labels on the inner box.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Ship the package">
    Use the **prepaid shipping label** provided with your RMA authorization. Drop off the package at the designated carrier location.

    For packages not using the prepaid label (e.g., non-warranty returns), ship to the address provided in your RMA authorization. VaultWares recommends using a trackable shipping service with insurance for high-value items.

    After shipping, update your support ticket with the **tracking number**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Track your RMA">
    Monitor the status of your RMA at [portal.vaultwares.com/support/rma](https://portal.vaultwares.com/support/rma) or via the CLI:

    ```bash
    # Check RMA status
    vw-admin rma status --rma-number RMA-20241115-0042

    # Output:
    # RMA number:   RMA-20241115-0042
    # Status:       In transit to VaultWares
    # Tracking:     1Z999AA10123456784
    # Received:     Pending
    # Resolution:   Replacement shipment
    # ETA:          2024-11-22 (estimated)
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## RMA resolution types

| Resolution | Description | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| **Advanced replacement** | Replacement device ships before return is received (requires credit card hold) | Next business day |
| **Standard replacement** | Replacement ships after defective unit is received and inspected | 5–10 business days after receipt |
| **Repair** | Device is repaired and returned | 10–15 business days after receipt |
| **Refund** | Available within 30 days of purchase for DOA units | 5–10 business days after receipt |
| **Certificate of Destruction** | Physical destruction with written certification | 5 business days after receipt |

## Advanced replacement

Advanced replacement ships a new device immediately while you return the defective unit.

- Requires a **valid credit card** on file (charged only if the defective unit is not returned within 30 days)
- Available for VaultDrive Pro/Enterprise, VaultHSM PCIe/Network, and VaultScan
- Request advanced replacement when opening your support ticket

## Shipping and logistics

| Situation | Shipping cost |
|---|---|
| Warranty defect (within warranty period) | VaultWares pays return shipping |
| DOA within 30 days of purchase | VaultWares pays return shipping |
| Non-warranty return or exchange | Customer pays return shipping |
| Advanced replacement outbound | VaultWares pays outbound shipping |

VaultWares ships replacements via FedEx Priority, UPS Next Day Air, or equivalent carrier depending on region. Signature required on delivery for HSM products.

## International RMAs

For customers outside the continental US:

- International return shipping labels are provided for warranty claims
- Customs documentation is handled by VaultWares — do not declare a commercial value on the customs form beyond what VaultWares provides
- Allow additional 5–10 business days for international RMAs
- Contact [support@vaultwares.com](mailto:support@vaultwares.com) to confirm regional availability for advanced replacement
